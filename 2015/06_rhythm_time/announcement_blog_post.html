The second in our mini-series investigating some fundamental elements of music: Melody & Harmony and Rhythm & Time. We'll start the day with talks exploring each of these subjects from the point of view of science, musicology, and art, then it's up to you to "hack" together your own project of any kind related to the basic elements of music.
